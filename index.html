Privacy Policy for SplitUp App

Last updated: March 11, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SplitUp! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information to function properly:

  • Photos from your device's photo library (only with your explicit permission)
  • Goals and progress data you create within the app
  • App preferences and settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Displaying and processing photos within the app
  • Saving your goals and progress
  • Maintaining your app preferences
  • Improving app functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your privacy and data security:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload your photos or data to any servers
  • Your information never leaves your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security features

5. Photo Library Access

Regarding photo access:

  • We request photo library access only when you choose to select a photo
  • You can deny or revoke access at any time in your device settings
  • We never access your photos without your explicit permission

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Delete any or all of your data at any time
  • Control photo library access permissions
  • Opt out of any future data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Our app:

  • Does not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • Is not targeted at children
  • Complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notifying you through app updates when possible
